# Double Angles

Here are all double angle formulas:

## Sine Double Angle:

$$
sin(2θ) = 2 sin(θ) cos(θ)
$$

## Cosine Double Angle:

$$
cos(2θ) = cos^2(θ) − sin^2(θ)\\
\quad = 2 cos^2(θ) − 1\\
\quad = 1 − 2 sin^2(θ)
$$

## Tangent Double Angle:

$$
tan(2θ) = \tfrac{sin(2θ)}{cos(2θ)}
$$
